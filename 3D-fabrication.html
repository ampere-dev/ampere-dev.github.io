Technical Build Specifications

Our advanced fabrication capabilities are designed to meet the rigorous demands of prototyping and small-batch production across industries such as aerospace, medical devices, automotive, and more. Below is a detailed overview of our technical specifications, ensuring precision, flexibility, and reliability for your projects.

Build Volume

Maximum Dimensions: Up to 500 x 500 x 500 mm (19.7 x 19.7 x 19.7 inches)

  • Our expansive build volume accommodates a wide range of component sizes, from intricate small parts to larger assemblies.
  • Ideal for producing complex prototypes, functional parts, or multi-component assemblies in a single build, reducing assembly time and ensuring dimensional consistency.

Material Options

Available Materials: ABS, PLA, Nylon, Aluminum (additional materials available upon request)

  • ABS (Acrylonitrile Butadiene Styrene): Durable, impact-resistant thermoplastic suitable for functional prototypes and end-use parts with moderate heat resistance.
  • PLA (Polylactic Acid): Eco-friendly, biodegradable material ideal for high-detail prototypes and non-functional display models.
  • Nylon: High-strength, flexible material with excellent wear and chemical resistance, perfect for mechanical components and dynamic applications.
  • Aluminum: Lightweight, corrosion-resistant metal for precision CNC-machined parts, offering superior strength-to-weight ratio for aerospace and automotive applications.
  • Custom material options can be sourced to meet specific project requirements, ensuring compatibility with your application’s mechanical, thermal, or aesthetic needs.

Resolution

Layer Thickness: ±0.1 mm (100 microns)

  • Our high-resolution 3D printing and CNC machining processes achieve fine layer thicknesses, delivering smooth surface finishes and intricate details.
  • Precision within ±0.1 mm ensures tight tolerances for functional components, minimizing post-processing and enabling seamless integration into assemblies.
  • Suitable for applications requiring high dimensional accuracy, such as medical device components or aerospace fittings.

Lead Time

Prototype Delivery: 3-7 business days (dependent on project complexity and material selection)

  • Streamlined workflows and advanced machinery enable rapid production, ensuring prototypes are delivered quickly to accelerate your development cycle.
  • Small-batch production lead times are optimized for efficiency, with expedited options available for urgent projects.
  • Real-time project tracking and dedicated support keep you informed, ensuring on-time delivery without compromising quality.
