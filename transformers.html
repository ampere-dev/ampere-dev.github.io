Transformer 101: Educational Resources

What Are Electrical Transformers?

Transformers transfer electrical energy between circuits through electromagnetic induction, stepping voltage up or down for efficient distribution. They are essential in power grids, reducing losses over distances.

Remanufactured vs. New: What's the Difference?

Remanufactured transformers are restored used units that meet original specs, offering cost savings (up to 50%) and shorter lead times. Ideal for bypassing supply chain delays while maintaining quality.

How Tariffs Impact Your Supply Chain

Tariffs on materials like steel can raise costs by 25%. Source domestically or choose reman options to mitigate. Calculate ROI: Reman can save thousands over time.
