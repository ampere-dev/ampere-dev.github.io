Is Your Three-Phase System Out of Balance?

Three-phase power is the foundation of modern industry, utilities, data centers, and critical infrastructure. Even small imbalances in voltage or current can cause overheating, reduced efficiency, nuisance tripping, or premature equipment failure — leading to unplanned downtime and expensive repairs.

Wye-configured load banks are the gold-standard tool for safely simulating balanced three-phase loads in wye/star systems (with neutral). They let you validate generators, transformers, UPS, switchgear, and distribution networks under real-world conditions without risking live equipment. Buying high-capacity wye banks is a major capital expense; Ampere Development’s rental fleet delivers immediate, professional-grade solutions with full setup support.
