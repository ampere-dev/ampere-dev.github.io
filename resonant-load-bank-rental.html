Are Your High-Frequency Power Systems Untested?

Resonant circuits in aerospace, defense, radar, avionics, and next-gen power electronics operate at precise frequencies where even minor mismatches can cause instability, overheating, harmonic distortion, or catastrophic failure under load.

Standard load banks cannot replicate true resonant behavior. Resonant load banks with tunable inductance and capacitance let you safely simulate real-world resonant conditions, validate frequency response, and prove system stability before deployment.
