Cookie Policy

Understanding Our Use of Cookies

This policy outlines how Ampere Development uses cookies and similar technologies to provide a seamless and personalized website experience.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device (e.g., computer, smartphone) when you visit our website. They help us remember your preferences, analyze site usage, and improve our services.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

We use the following types of cookies:

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for the website to function (e.g., enabling navigation, accessing secure areas). These cannot be disabled.
  • Performance Cookies: Collect anonymous data about how you use our site (e.g., pages visited, time spent) to improve performance.
  • Functional Cookies: Remember your preferences (e.g., language, region) for a personalized experience.
  • Marketing Cookies: Track your browsing habits to deliver relevant ads (used only with your consent).

3. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies to:

  • Ensure our website functions properly (e.g., navigating between pages).
  • Analyze site usage to optimize performance and user experience.
  • Personalize content, such as remembering your preferences or quote requests.
  • Provide targeted marketing (with your consent; opt out via privacy@ampere.dev).

4. Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through:

  • Browser Settings: Adjust your browser to block or delete cookies (see your browser’s help section).
  • Our Cookie Banner: Choose your preferences when you first visit our site.
  • Opt-Out: Contact privacy@ampere.dev to opt out of non-essential cookies.

Note: Disabling cookies may limit some website functionality.

5. Third-Party Cookies

We use trusted third-party services (e.g., Google Analytics) that may set cookies to analyze site performance. These providers are bound by strict data protection agreements. You can opt out of these cookies via our cookie banner or browser settings.

6. Changes to This Policy

Effective Date: May 23, 2025. We may update this policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Updates will be posted here.
