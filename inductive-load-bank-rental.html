What Are Inductive Load Banks?

Inductive load banks simulate reactive electrical loads to test the performance and stability of power systems under conditions that mimic real-world inductive loads, such as motors and transformers. They introduce a lagging power factor, allowing you to verify the efficiency and reliability of generators, UPS systems, and other power equipment.
