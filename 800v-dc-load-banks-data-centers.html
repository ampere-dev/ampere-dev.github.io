The 5 Levels of Data Center Commissioning

800V DC load banks are essential in Levels 4 and 5, enabling realistic full-load simulation of AI/GPU workloads and complete HVDC power-chain validation before live deployment.

Levels 1–3: Factory & Component Testing

Verification without significant load:

  • Level 1: Factory witness testing of rectifiers, PDUs, UPS
  • Level 2: Pre-installation inspections and static checks
  • Level 3: Initial energization and basic startup

Load banks rarely used here.

Level 4: Functional Performance Testing (FPT)

Key Role for 800V DC Load Banks

Progressive stepped loads (25–110%) on subsystems.

  • HVDC rectifier regulation, ripple, and stability
  • UPS/battery runtime and recharge performance
  • PDU thermal and breaker coordination
  • Cooling system response validation

Rackmount 800V DC units are ideal for precise subsystem testing.

Level 5: Integrated Systems Testing (IST)

Essential Role for 800V DC Load Banks

Full end-to-end 100%+ design load for 24–72+ hours with failure scenarios.

  • Real AI/GPU rack load emulation across the entire facility
  • Generator, UPS, and automatic failover under sustained load
  • PUE, temperature stability, and resiliency testing
  • Uptime Institute Tier certification support

Networked, programmable 800V DC load banks make this phase possible at hyperscale.
