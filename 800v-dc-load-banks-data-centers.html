The 5 Levels of Data Center Commissioning Testing

Industry-standard commissioning follows five progressive levels. 800V DC load banks play a pivotal role in Levels 4 and 5, enabling full-load simulation for true system validation and ensuring reliable, efficient performance before live operations.

Level 1–3: Factory, Pre-Installation & Startup

Component verification, site inspections, and initial energization with minimal or no load. Focus: manufacturing quality, wiring integrity, and basic functionality.

Level 4: Functional Performance Testing

Key Role for 800V DC Load Banks

Individual subsystems are tested under stepped loads (25–100%). Validates UPS/battery runtime, rectifiers, PDUs, voltage regulation, and thermal performance in the 800V DC environment.

Level 5: Integrated Systems Testing (IST)

Essential Role for 800V DC Load Banks

Full facility simulation at 100%+ load for extended periods. Tests end-to-end resilience, failover, cooling response, and PUE under real-world AI workloads—ensuring 99.999% uptime readiness.
