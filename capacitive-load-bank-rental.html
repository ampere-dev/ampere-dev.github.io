What Are Capacitive Load Banks?

Capacitive load banks simulate reactive electrical loads with a leading power factor, commonly found in systems with capacitors or electronics. They are used to test the performance and stability of power systems under conditions that mimic capacitive loads, ensuring generators, UPS systems, and other equipment operate efficiently.
