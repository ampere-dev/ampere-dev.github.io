Starline Paddle Repair

Restore Your Starline Systems with Confidence

Whether caused by arc-flash incidents, shipping damage, or wear and tear, we offer reliable and durable replacements to restore your system’s performance and ensure it runs at its best.

Developed Solution - Patent Pending

  • Material Innovation: Crafted from advanced thermoplastics, our solution combines impact resistance, rigidity, and lightweight design, ensuring durability under wear, impact, and challenging environmental conditions.
  • Precision Manufacturing: Produced using state-of-the-art Fused Deposition Modeling (FDM), offering enhanced strength and resilience compared to traditional injection molding. FDM technology ensures exceptional layer adhesion and material integrity for long-lasting performance.
  • Built to Endure: Designed to withstand high mechanical stress, with machine-grade screws providing added stability and robust assembly.
  • Performance and Reliability: Manufactured with robust materials and advanced processes to deliver consistent, dependable performance, even in demanding environments.
  • Customizable and Accessible: Available in a range of color options to match system aesthetics. Components are easily sourced, eliminating delays typically experienced with OEM parts. The design also supports flexibility in materials while maintaining a fixed form to meet system-specific requirements.
  • Cost-Effective Flexibility: Allows for cost-efficient customization without compromising quality, enabling tailored solutions to match unique system needs.

Technical Specifications

  • Continuous Use Temperature (CUT): 50°C (122°F)
  • Tensile Strength XY axis: 50 MPa (7,252 PSI)
    • Tensile Strength Z axis: 37 MPa (5,366 PSI)
