Are Harmonic Loads Threatening Your Power Quality?

Today’s facilities are filled with non-linear loads — servers, VFDs, UPS systems, LED lighting, and electronics — that inject harmful harmonics into the power network. These can cause overheating, nuisance tripping, reduced equipment life, and even complete system failure.

Standard linear load banks cannot replicate these real-world conditions. Non-linear load banks let you safely simulate harmonic-rich loads to validate power quality, THD levels, and system resilience. Purchasing specialized non-linear equipment is expensive and rarely needed full-time.
