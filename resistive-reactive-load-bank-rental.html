Are Unreliable Power Systems Costing You?

Real-world loads are never purely resistive — motors, transformers, and electronics create both resistive and reactive (inductive or capacitive) demands. Testing with only resistive banks gives incomplete results and leaves systems vulnerable to failures, inefficiency, or unexpected trips.

Resistive-reactive load banks simulate true combined loads, letting you validate power factor, voltage regulation, and system stability under realistic conditions.
