Starline Tap Box Rental Solutions

Our Starline Tapboxes, also known as starline tap box rentals, are precision-engineered for seamless integration with B400T5 series busways, providing compact, safe, and high-performance power solutions. Designed for temporary or permanent setups in data centers, manufacturing facilities, and other critical environments, these units also support load bank testing with robust terminal block configurations.

  • Universal compatibility with B400T5 busways for versatile applications.
  • Rated for 480V and 400A, ideal for data centers and manufacturing.
  • Lightweight (40 lbs.) and compact (8 x 12 x 20 inches) for standard units; terminal block units measure 12 x 8 x 25 inches and weigh 46 lbs.
  • Meets NFPA 70E safety standards for secure operation.
  • Replacement parts available; call to discuss your needs.
  • Durable painted steel enclosure with color-coded female camlock connectors and snap covers for safe load bank connections.
  • IP54-rated enclosure for dust and splash resistance in varied environments.
  • Tool-free installation for rapid and straightforward setup.
  • High-efficiency design for energy savings and optimal thermal performance.
  • Includes neutral and ground connections, supporting all busway configurations for load testing and commissioning.
