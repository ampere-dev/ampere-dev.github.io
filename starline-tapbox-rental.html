Tap Box Paddle Commissioning

Starline Tapbox Rental

Tap-off boxes are essential for drawing power from a busway system. These boxes feature a specialized plug head, commonly referred to as a paddle, which is inserted into the busway track slot. Once inserted, the tap-off box is twisted 90 degrees to establish an electrical connection. This action engages the paddle assembly blades with the busbar channel inside the busway track, forming a secure compression connection.

These tap-off boxes facilitate overhead power distribution networks by providing a seamless connection system. They are especially valuable for commissioning busways and verifying system operation when paired with a load bank and cables.

Key Features and Specifications

  • Compatibility: Designed for use with the B400T5 series busway.
  • Electrical Rating: 600V, 400A.
  • Compact and Portable:
  • Weight: 40 lbs.
  • Dimensions: 8 x 12 x 20 inches (L x W x H).

Safety Guidelines

To ensure maximum safety during installation and removal of the tap-off boxes:

  • Adhere to NFPA 70E standards for electrical safety in the workplace.
  • Properly assess and mitigate arc flash and electrical hazards before engaging with the system.
  • Use appropriate personal protective equipment (PPE) and tools during all handling procedures.

Note: Always ensure the busway power distribution system is de-energized before connecting or disconnecting a tap-off box to prevent accidents.
