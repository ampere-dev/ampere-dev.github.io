Are Reactive Loads Jeopardizing Your Power Reliability?

Real-world power systems rarely see purely resistive loads. Motors, transformers, capacitors, and electronics create lagging or leading reactive loads that can cause voltage instability, overheating, reduced efficiency, or complete system failure if not properly tested.

Reactive load banks let you safely simulate these exact conditions to validate generators, UPS, switchgear, and distribution networks under realistic power factor scenarios.
