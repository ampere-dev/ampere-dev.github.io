Trust and Transparency

At Ampere Development, we are committed to fostering trust through transparent practices and safeguarding your privacy. Privacy Policy

Protecting Your Information

Your privacy is a top priority. This section explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal and business information when you interact with Ampere Development.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide and improve our load bank rentals, Starline tapbox rentals, and load testing services. This may include:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, and billing details provided when you request a quote, place an order, or contact us.
  • Business Information: Company name, address, and industry details to tailor our services to your needs.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with our website, such as IP addresses, browser types, and pages visited, collected via cookies and analytics tools.
  • Service-Related Data: Technical details about the equipment rented or tested, such as load bank specifications or test results, to ensure proper service delivery.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Process rental agreements and service requests.
  • Communicate with you about your orders, updates, or support inquiries.
  • Improve our website, services, and customer experience through analytics.
  • Comply with legal obligations, such as tax reporting or safety regulations.
  • Send optional marketing communications (with your consent, and you can opt out at any time).

3. Data Protection

We implement robust security measures to protect your data, including:

  • Encryption of sensitive information during transmission and storage.
  • Access controls to limit who can view your data within our organization.
  • Regular security audits to identify and address vulnerabilities.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties except in the following cases:

  • Service Providers: Trusted partners who assist with payment processing, equipment delivery, or website analytics, bound by strict confidentiality agreements.
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, such as in response to a court order or regulatory request.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger or acquisition, your data may be transferred to the new entity under the same privacy protections.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you.
  • Request corrections to inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Request deletion of your data (subject to legal retention requirements).
  • Opt out of marketing communications at any time.

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@ampere.dev. We will respond promptly in accordance with applicable data protection laws.

6. Cookies and Tracking

Our website uses cookies to enhance your experience. You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings. For details, see our Cookie Policy.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically to reflect changes in our services or legal requirements. Changes will be posted here. Effective Date: May 23, 2025
