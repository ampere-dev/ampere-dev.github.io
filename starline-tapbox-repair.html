Professional Repair Process

We specialize in repairing Starline TBT5G series tapboxes damaged during installation, load testing, or normal use. Our technicians diagnose, repair, test, and certify your unit for safe return to service.

  • Repair or replacement of damaged female camlock connectors
  • Enclosure repair or replacement (cracks, dents, paint)
  • Stab and mounting tab straightening or replacement
  • Directional indicator and safety label restoration
  • Full electrical continuity and hi-pot testing
  • Cleaning and corrosion removal
  • IP54 seal restoration
  • 6-month warranty on all repairs
  • Detailed repair report provided
  • Fast 3–7 business day turnaround
